Granola & Yogurt Bowl

Because every morning needs to start off with a bang! And these granola & yogurt bowls do just that. So yummy, so delicious, and they each only take about 5 minutes to make!

This recipe yields 1 serving.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup plain greek yogurt
  • ¼ cup blueberries
  • ¼ cup raspberries
  • ½ cup cantaloupe
  • ½ Tbsp goji berries
  • ½ Tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • ½ Tbsp slices almonds
  • ½ Tbsp coconut shavings
  • ¼ cup granola

Steps

  1. Place the yogurt in a bowl.
  2. Add the washed fruit.
  3. Top with seeds, nuts, coconut, and granola.
  4. Serve immediately.
