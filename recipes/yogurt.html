Granola & Yogurt Bowl

Description

Because every morning needs to start off with a bang! And these granola & yogurt bowls do just that. So yummy, so delicious, and you can make them in a jiffy!

  • Level: Easy
  • Total time: 5 minutes
  • Preparation time: 5 minutes
  • Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • ½ cup plain greek yogurt
  • ¼ cup blueberries
  • ¼ cup raspberries
  • ½ cup cantaloupe
  • ½ Tbsp. goji berries
  • ½ Tbsp. pumpkin seeds
  • ½ Tbsp. slices almonds
  • ½ Tbsp. coconut shavings
  • ¼ cup granola

Steps

  1. Place the yogurt in a bowl.
  2. Add the washed fruit.
  3. Top with seeds, nuts, coconut, and granola.
  4. Serve immediately.
