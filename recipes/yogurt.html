Granola & Yogurt Bowl

Description

Because every morning needs to start off with a bang! And this granola and yogurt bowl does just that. So yummy, so delicious, and you can make it in a jiffy!

  • Level: Easy
  • Total time: 5 minutes
  • Preparation time: 5 minutes
  • Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients

Steps

  1. Place the yogurt in a bowl.
  2. Add the washed fruit.
  3. Top with seeds, nuts, coconut, and granola.
  4. Serve immediately.
