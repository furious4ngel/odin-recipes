Sriracha Noodles

Description

A simple yet absolutely flavor-packed Thai noodles dish — for those who aren't afraid of a bit of heat.

Recipe details for sriracha noodles
Level Easy
Total Time 20 minutes
Preparation Time 10 minutes
Yield 4 servings

Ingredients

Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook rice noodles in salted water until al dente (do not overcook them). Drain.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet and sauté garlic and ginger over medium heat for about 1 minute, stirring frequently. Add hot chili pepper, red sweet pepper, the whites of the spring onions, salt, pepper, and smoked paprika. Sauté for about 4 minutes, stirring frequently. Also, stir in tomato paste and sriracha.
  3. To make the sauce, simply add all sauce ingredients to a medium bowl and stir with a whisk.
  4. Pour the sauce into the pan and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let simmer for about 1 minute.
  5. Add the drained noodles and toss to combine with the sauce. Cook for a further 1 minute, taste it and adjust seasonings if needed.
  6. Garnish with the greens of the spring onions and sesame seeds, serve, and enjoy!
