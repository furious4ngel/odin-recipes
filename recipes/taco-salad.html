Taco Salad

Description

This dish is packed with flavor from the turkey taco meat, which includes roasted peppers, taco seasoning and salsa.

Recipe details for taco salad
Level Medium
Total Time 55 minutes
Preparation Time 25 minutes
Yield 3 servings

Ingredients

Turkey Taco Meat

  • 12 mini bell peppers
  • Olive oil or avocado oil cooking spray
  • ½ Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 lb. ground turkey
  • 2 Tbsp. taco seasoning
  • 2 Tbsp. salsa

Salad

  • 1 large head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • ½ cup grape or cherry tomatoes, chopped
  • ½ cup sweet corn, thawed
  • ¼ cup shredded Mexican cheese
  • ½ jalapeño pepper, de-seeded and chopped
  • 1 avocado, chopped into chunks
  • ¼ cup tortilla chips, broken into pieces
  • Chopped cilantro, for garnish
  • Hot sauce (optional)
  • Salsa, for taste

Creamy Southwest Dressing

  • ½ cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp. lime juice
  • ⅛ tsp. cumin
  • ¼ tsp. chili powder
  • ¼ tsp. garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp. salt
  • ¼ tsp. pepper
  • ¼ cup salsa
  • 2 Tbsp. cilantro

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Place peppers on a baking sheet, spray with olive or avocado oil cooking spray, sprinkle on a little salt and pepper and place in the oven to roast for 20 to 30 minutes, or until peppers are soft. Let peppers cool, remove stems and any large seeds and then dice into small pieces. The diced up peppers should measure out to be about ⅓ cup.
  2. Add all ingredients for the dressing into a blender and blend until fully combined and smooth. You can also use an immersion blender for this.
  3. Add oil to a skillet over medium heat. Once hot, add turkey. Break meat into small pieces using a spatula or meat chopper as it cooks. Cook for about 6 to 8 minutes or until the turkey is no longer pink. Add taco seasoning, salsa and roasted peppers to the pan and toss to combine. Set aside.
  4. While the turkey is cooking, start to prep your salad ingredients. To build salads, start with a base of romaine lettuce in each bowl and add the toppings: turkey taco meat, tomatoes, corn, cheese, jalapeño, avocado and chips.
  5. Drizzle a little dressing over each salad and serve with chopped cilantro and extra dressing. Add some salsa and a few drops of hot sauce, if using. Another option is to place all the salad ingredients into a large salad bowl and toss with dressing, then portion into 2 to 4 bowls for serving.
