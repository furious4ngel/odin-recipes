Chicken Enchiladas

Description

Discover chicken enchiladas, a recipe that features cream cheese, lots of delicious herbs and spices and saucy chicken.

Recipe details for chicken enchiladas
Level Medium
Total Time 40 minutes
Preparation Time 20 minutes
Yield 6 servings

Ingredients

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix cream cheese spread, 2 Tbsp. milk and garlic powder until blended.
  3. Heat oil in large skillet on medium heat. Add peppers, onions and garlic; cook and stir 5 to 6 minutes, or until crisp-tender. Stir in chicken, ¾ cup each cream cheese mixture and shredded cheese, and ¼ cup enchilada sauce.
  4. Dip 1 tortilla in remaining enchilada sauce; gently shake off excess sauce. Spoon ¼ cup chicken mixture down center of tortilla; roll up. Place, seam side down, in 13-by-9-in. baking dish sprayed with cooking spray. Repeat with remaining tortillas and filling; cover with remaining sauce.
  5. Bake 20 minutes. Top with remaining shredded cheese; bake 5 minutes. Mix remaining cream cheese mixture and remaining milk; spoon over enchiladas.
