Orange Chicken & Asparagus

Description

For a healthier alternative to a favorite Chinese dish, try orange chicken and asparagus. Serve over rice or noodles, or enjoy on its own!

Recipe details for orange chicken and asparagus
Level Easy
Total Time 15 minutes
Preparation Time 5 minutes
Yield 1 serving

Ingredients

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet. Sauté sliced chicken, garlic, and red pepper flakes for about 7 minutes. Add chopped asparagus and cook for an additional 3 minutes.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, sesame seeds, and juice and zest of 1 orange. Add to skillet and cook for 1 minute. Serve plain or over rice of your choice.
